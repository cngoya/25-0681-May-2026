About Us

Founded in the heart of Nairobi, Bloom & Petal Haven began with a simple belief: that everyone deserves a little piece of nature in their daily lives. What started as a small stall at a local market has grown into one of the city's most trusted flower and plant shops, serving homes, offices, and special occasions across the region.

Our team of passionate florists and horticulturists hand-select every stem and leaf, ensuring that what arrives at your door is nothing short of perfect. We work directly with local farmers to support the community and bring you the freshest blooms possible.

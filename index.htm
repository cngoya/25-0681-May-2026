Welcome to Bloom & Petal Haven

Welcome to Bloom & Petal Haven, Nairobi's most beloved online flower and plant shop, where nature meets your doorstep.
Whether you are looking to brighten up your living room with a bouquet of fresh roses, transform your balcony into a lush green escape, or fill your office with the calming scent of lavender, we have everything you need to bring beauty into every corner of your life.
Our flowers are handpicked daily to ensure maximum freshness, and our indoor plants are carefully selected to thrive in Nairobi's unique climate.
From seed to delivery, Bloom & Petal Haven is your trusted partner in growing, gifting, and celebrating life through flowers.

  3. Feed with fertilizer monthly
  4. Prune dead leaves regularly
  5. Repot as the plant grows
